### 1. `area_currents` (1704 of 2948)

*Displays a detailed map of Highclere Estate and surrounding areas mapping the Belinus Current and the Elen Current.*

* **The Belinus Current (Gold):** Enters from the southeast, flowing South to North through Highclere Estate.
* **Entry Point:** Beacon Hill
* **Path:** Passes through the *Beacon Hill* node, runs north through the node *Bowl Barrow*. As it enters into Highclere Estate due northwest, it then passes through *Monk's Garden* and the *Folly* node before curving due northeast and reaches *The Temple* node close to the Mere lake. It exits continuing north past west of Milford Lake.
* **Nodes Visited (5):** Beacon Hill, Bowl Barrow, Monk's Garden, Folly, The Temple

* **The Elen Current (Purple):** Flows South to North through Highclere Castle, starting near the *Car Park* southeast before reaching Beacon Hill.
* **Entry Point:** Beacon Hill
* **Path:** Heads northwest through *Heaven's Gate* at the foot of *Sidown Hill*, then continues up North through *Highclere Castle* before reaching *Cemetery Chapel* near a Dairy Farm. Then it continues up north through a church called *St Michael and All Angels* before exiting.
* **Nodes Visited (6):** Beacon Hill, Heaven's Gate, Highclere Castle, Cemetery Chapel, Dairy Farm and St Michael and All Angels

* **Intersection Points:** The two currents meet at **Beacon Hill**.
* **Auxiliary Nodes:** Highclere stud and Car Park are auxiliary markers and do not lie on either current.

### 4. `area_currents` (1993 of 2948)

*Displays a detailed map of Uffington area, including important nodes such as Uffington Castle and Dragon Hill mapping the Belinus Current and the Elen Current.*

* **The Belinus Current (Gold):** Enters from the southeast, flowing South to North from Uffington Castle. The current emerges from Idlebush Barrow.
* **Entry Point:** Uffington Castle
* **Path:** Passes through the *Spring* node, north of the Castle, runs north through the *Dragon Hill* node. The Belinus current exits northeasat toward Farringdon Folly.
* **Nodes Visited (3):** Uffington Castle, Spring and Dragon Hill

* **The Elen Current (Purple):** Flows South to North through Uffington Castle, emerging from Wayland Smithy.
* **Entry Point:** Uffington Castle
* **Path:** Heads northeast through *Prehistoric Burial Site*, then continues up North through *Uffington White Horse* before reaching *Dragon Hill*. Then it continues northwest through *Woolstone Springs* and exits toward All Saints Church Woolstone.
* **Nodes Visited (5):** Uffington Castle, Prehistoric Burial Site, Uffington White Horse, Dragon Hill and Woolstone Springs.

* **Intersection Points:** The two currents meet at **Uffington Castle** and **Dragon Hill**.
* **Auxiliary Nodes:** The Manger (a long narrow dry valley, which stretches for half a kilometre north-west of White Horse Hill) is an auxiliary marker. A part of it lies on the Elen Current, from Dragon Hill to Woolstone Springs.
